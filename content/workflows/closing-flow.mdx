# Closing Workflow

This guide covers the closing process in JaroLoan from Clear to Close (CTC) through funding.

## Clear to Close

### CTC Requirements

Before marking Clear to Close:

- [ ] All underwriting conditions cleared
- [ ] Final approval obtained
- [ ] Insurance verified
- [ ] Title cleared

### CTC Process

1. Final condition review
2. Click **Clear to Close**
3. System validates requirements
4. Loan moves to CTC stage

## Closing Disclosure (CD)

### CD Generation

1. Navigate to **Disclosures** tab
2. Click **Generate CD**
3. Review all figures
4. Confirm LE/CD comparison

### 3-Day Waiting Period

The CD must be received 3 business days before closing:

- **Business days** exclude Sundays and federal holidays
- **Receipt** depends on delivery method:
  - Electronic: same day
  - Mail: 3 days assumed
  - In person: same day

### CD Revisions

Certain changes require a new 3-day wait:

- APR increases > 0.125% (fixed) or > 0.25% (adjustable)
- Loan product change
- Prepayment penalty added

Other changes require only a corrected CD.

## Tolerance Monitoring

JaroLoan tracks TRID tolerance compliance:

| Tolerance | Fees |
|-----------|------|
| **Zero** | Transfer taxes, fees paid to lender |
| **10%** | Third-party fees where lender selects provider |
| **None** | Fees borrower shops for, prepaid items |

### Cure Requirements

If tolerance exceeded:
- System flags violation
- Cure amount calculated
- Lender credit applied

## Closing Day

### Final Preparation

- Confirm closing figures
- Verify wire instructions
- Prepare closing package

### Document Signing

- Loan documents signed
- Notarization completed
- Documents returned

## Funding

### Pre-Funding Checklist

- [ ] All PTF conditions cleared
- [ ] Signed documents received
- [ ] Final figures balanced
- [ ] Wire instructions verified

### Funding Authorization

1. Navigate to **Funding** tab
2. Review final checklist
3. Click **Authorize Funding**
4. Record wire confirmation

### Recording

After funding:
- Deed recorded
- Recording info captured
- Loan moves to Funded stage

## Post-Close

- Final document collection
- Investor delivery preparation
- File completion audit
