# Disclosures

JaroLoan provides automated generation and management of TRID-compliant disclosures.

## Disclosure Types

### Loan Estimate (LE)

Initial disclosure sent within 3 business days of application:

- Loan terms summary
- Projected payments
- Estimated closing costs
- Comparisons (APR, TIP)

### Closing Disclosure (CD)

Final disclosure sent 3 business days before closing:

- Final loan terms
- Actual closing costs
- Cash to close
- LE comparison

## Generating Loan Estimate

### Initial LE

1. Complete loan application
2. Navigate to **Disclosures** tab
3. Click **Generate LE**
4. Review all figures
5. Click **Send to Borrower**

### Re-Disclosure

When changes require re-disclosure:

1. Update loan terms
2. System prompts for re-disclosure
3. Click **Generate Revised LE**
4. Document changed circumstance
5. Send to borrower

## Intent to Proceed

### Capturing Intent

After LE delivery, capture intent:

1. Borrower reviews LE
2. Indicates intent to proceed
3. Document in system:
   - Verbal (date/time/who)
   - Written (upload document)
   - Electronic (e-signature)

### Fee Collection

<Callout type="warning">

## TRID Timeline

### 3-Business-Day Rule (LE)

LE must be delivered within 3 business days of application:

- **Business days** = All calendar days except Sunday and federal holidays
- **Delivery** = Sent or placed in mail
- **Receipt** = 3 days after mailing if not electronic

### 3-Business-Day Rule (CD)

CD must be received 3 business days before closing:

- **Receipt** = Actual receipt for electronic
- **Closing** = Consummation date
- **Wait** = Cannot close until period expires

## Changed Circumstances

### Valid Changed Circumstances

Re-disclosure allowed for:

- Borrower-requested changes
- New information affecting eligibility
- Interest rate lock events
- Settlement provider changes
- Construction delays

### Documentation

When re-disclosing:

1. Document the circumstance
2. Note date discovered
3. Generate revised LE within 3 days
4. Keep audit trail

## Closing Disclosure

### Generating CD

1. Loan reaches CTC stage
2. Navigate to **Disclosures** tab
3. Click **Generate CD**
4. Review LE/CD comparison
5. Check tolerance compliance
6. Send to borrower

### CD Revisions

**Triggers new 3-day wait:**
- APR increases > 0.125% (fixed)
- APR increases > 0.25% (variable)
- Loan product change
- Prepayment penalty added

**No new waiting period:**
- Seller credits change
- Minor cost adjustments
- Clerical corrections

## Tolerance Tracking

### Zero Tolerance

No increase allowed:

- Lender fees
- Broker fees
- Affiliated service fees
- Transfer taxes

### 10% Cumulative Tolerance

10% aggregate increase allowed:

- Recording fees
- Third-party fees (lender-selected)

### No Tolerance Limit

Can change any amount:

- Borrower-shopped services
- Prepaid items
- Initial escrow
